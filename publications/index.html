Books

In 1996, ETH Zurich have organized a Short Course on “Three-Dimensional Velocity and Vorticity Measuring and Image Analysis Techniques”. The lectures of this course (including those of Th. Dracos, A. Grün, H.-G. Maas, among the others) were published as ERCOFTAC Series Vol. 4, by Kluwer Academic Publishers. The book (ISBN: 0-7923-4256-9) is available from Springer (that acquired Kluwer) or from Amazon.
